Frequently asked

Is this compliant with LinkedIn's terms?

Profile owners actively participate — they write and approve every message. This is a rental agreement between a professional and a business, not account sharing. We recommend businesses use HeyReach's own infrastructure for sending.

Who writes the outreach messages?

You do. Or your team. Profile owners aren't copywriters — they review messages for relevance and accuracy, then approve them. You maintain full control of the actual content.

How fast can I get access?

Most profile matches happen within 24 hours of your request. You can be running outreach under a new profile by tomorrow.

What industries/geographies are available?

We have profiles spanning tech, finance, healthcare, legal, and more — across Europe, US, and APAC. Filter by ICP when you submit your request.

What if a profile owner goes inactive?

We monitor active usage. If an owner becomes unresponsive, we match you to a replacement profile and offer a pro-rated credit for the downtime.
