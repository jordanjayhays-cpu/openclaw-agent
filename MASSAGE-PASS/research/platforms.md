## 1. Treatwell

**URL:** https://www.treatwell.es / https://www.treatwell.co.uk
**Coverage:** 14 countries (Spain, UK, France, Germany, Italy, Portugal, Netherlands, Ireland, Belgium, Austria, Switzerland, Greece, Lithuania)

### Key Features
- **Search + Filters:** By treatment type, location, price range, rating
- **Map View:** Toggle map on/off on listing page; shows pins for each venue
- **"Express Offers":** Last-minute deals highlighted separately
- **Trending on Socials:** Shows what treatments are popular (e.g., "Sports Massage", "Balayage")
- **Gift Cards:** Digital gift cards (14-digit code, wallet system, 12-month expiry, 14-day cancellation)
- **App:** 3-click booking claim, SMS reminders, calendar sync
- **"Top Rated" Program:** 5-Year Award winners highlighted prominently (social proof)
- **Corporate Gifting:** Bulk gift card orders (100+) via form

### Studio Profile Page Data
- **Rating:** Star rating (e.g., 5.0) + review count
- **Reviews:** Individual reviews with reviewer name, rating, text, venue response
- **Services:** Grouped by category (Facial, Massage, Body), each with duration + price
- **Photos:** Gallery of studio images
- **About:** Detailed venue description (often 100-200 words)
- **Team:** Individual therapist profiles with name, title, bio (e.g., "Paolo — Founder & Sports Massage Specialist")
- **Location:** Address + nearest metro/public transport
- **Opening Hours:** Day-by-day schedule
- **Payment:** Accepted methods (cash, credit card, debit card)
- **Languages:** Spoken languages listed
- **Brands:** Product brands used (e.g., iS CLINICAL, Indiba)
- **Amenities:** Sauna, private rooms, etc.
- **"Lo que nos gusta del lugar":** Platform's curated highlights (atmosphere, specialization)

### Social Proof Presentation
- **"546 centros que ofrecen Masajes en Madrid"** — massive venue count
- **"5 años siendo Top Rated"** — anniversary badge
- **Review counts prominently displayed** — e.g., "5,029 opiniones"
- **% savings displayed** — e.g., "Ahorro de hasta un 25%"
- **"Nuevo"** badges for recently added venues

### Booking Flow (Step by Step)
1. Search by location or browse by category
2. Filter by treatment type, price, rating
3. View venue cards (photo, name, rating, review count, price range, distance)
4. Click venue → Full profile page with services list
5. Select service → see available time slots (calendar view)
6. Add to cart / book
7. Checkout with login (email or social)
8. Payment (credit card or gift card balance)
9. Confirmation screen + email

### Pricing Model
- **Commission-based:** Treatwell takes a commission per booking (venue pays)
- **Featured Placement:** Venues can pay for promoted positioning
- **Gift Cards:** Platform takes % on gift card redemptions
- **No upfront cost for venues** — performance-based

### Design & UI Patterns
- **Colors:** White, coral/orange accent (#FF6B6B vibe), soft grays
- **Cards:** White cards with soft shadows, rounded corners, large cover photo
- **Typography:** Clean sans-serif, generous white space
- **Map:** Google Maps integration with custom pins
- **Navigation:** Top nav with category mega-menu
- **Trust signals:** SSL badges, review stars, verified venue badges

### What Makes Them Look Legitimate
- Multi-language support (14 country versions)
- 5-year anniversary campaign ("5 años Top Rated")
- Huge venue counts ("thousands of venues")
- Professional venue photography required
- Detailed venue descriptions
- Owner responses to reviews
- Gift card system with formal policies
- Corporate gifting program
- Established company (founded 2008 as Wahanda)

## 2. Fresha

**URL:** https://www.fresha.com
**Coverage:** Global (UK, US, Australia, Canada, Ireland, Singapore, UAE, South Africa, New Zealand + more)

### Key Features
- **Instant Booking:** No approval needed — real-time availability
- **No Credit Card at Booking:** Pay at venue (reduces friction)
- **Free for Businesses:** Platform is 100% free for venues (no commission claimed in marketing)
- **Ratings & Reviews:** After-visit review collection
- **Reminder System:** SMS reminders to reduce no-shows
- **Consultation Forms:** Intake forms before appointments
- **Wallet System:** Save payment methods for faster checkout
- **Multi-category:** Hair, beauty, nails, massage, fitness, tattoos

### Studio Profile Page Data
- Venue name, cover photo
- Star rating
- Services list with duration + price
- Location
- Availability calendar
- Reviews
- Team/stylist info

### Social Proof Presentation
- Testimonials prominently displayed on homepage from users globally
- "100,000+ businesses" claim
- "Free forever" pricing — removes barrier for venues
- 5-star ratings from users in specific cities

### Pricing Model
- **Free for businesses** (key differentiator)
- Revenue from payment processing (Stripe integration) on transactions
- Featured/promoted listings

### Design & UI Patterns
- **Colors:** White, teal/mint accent (#00C9A7 vibe)
- **Layout:** Clean, card-based, mobile-first
- **Trust:** App-store style star badges, review snippets

### What Makes Them Look Legitimate
- "Free forever" removes business resistance
- Global testimonials in specific cities
- Clean, modern app feel
- Payment integration (Stripe) signals real business

## 5. Bark.com

**URL:** https://www.bark.com
**Coverage:** UK + US (expanding)

### Key Features
- **Lead Generation (NOT Booking):** Customers submit requests, professionals contact them
- **Request System:** Describe what you need, get quotes from pros
- **Verified Reviews:** Reviews from actual customers
- **Category Pages:** Massage therapy, personal trainers, therapists, etc.
- **Pro Directory:** Search for local professionals
- **No upfront fees** for customers

### Studio/Professional Profile Data
- Name and photo
- Rating + reviews
- Services offered
- Location
- Response time
- About section

### Pricing Model
- **Lead-based:** Professionals pay per lead/contact (not per booking)
- Credit pack system (buy credits, spend on leads)
- Featured pro placement

### Booking Flow
1. Customer posts request ("I need a deep tissue massage near Madrid")
2. Professionals who match are notified
3. Professionals contact the customer directly
4. Customer chooses provider
5. Booking happens off-platform

### Design & UI Patterns
- Color: Orange accent (#F97316)
- Simple, high-conversion landing pages
- Testimonial-heavy

### What Makes Them Look Legitimate
- Active since 2014
- Massive lead volume (millions of requests)
- Detailed testimonials with names and cities
- Trustpilot reviews

## Key Design Patterns Observed

### Cards (All Platforms)
- Large cover photo (16:9 or square)
- Star rating + review count
- Venue name + specialty tags
- Price range displayed
- Distance/location
- Soft shadows, white backgrounds

### Map Integration
- Google Maps with custom pins
- Color-coded pins by category
- Cluster markers when zoomed out
- "Show/Hide map" toggle

### Trust Signals
- Review counts (not just stars)
- "Verified" or "New" badges
- % savings displayed
- "X years in business" claims
- Professional photography requirements

### Booking Flow Commonality
1. Search/Filter → 2. Venue Cards → 3. Venue Profile → 4. Service Selection → 5. Time Slot → 6. Login/Checkout → 7. Confirmation

## Recommendations for Massage Pass

### Feature Priorities
1. **Map + Cards** — treatwell-style with toggle
2. **Real Studio Profiles** — photos, reviews, team, services, hours
3. **Instant Booking** — no approval step (reduces friction)
4. **QR Stamp System** — differentiated walk-in loyalty
5. **Reviews** — verified after-visit reviews
6. **Gift Cards** — digital wallet system

### Trust-Building Design
- Display real Madrid addresses and metro proximity
- Use actual review counts (even if small at first)
- Show "X studios in Madrid" count prominently
- Professional studio photography requirements
- Individual therapist profiles with photos
- Response capability for venues (owner replies to reviews)

### Pricing Recommendation
- Commission-based (like Treatwell) — align incentives
- €2-5 per booking to start
- Walk-in stamp revenue (€0.50 per stamp activation)
- Featured placement for €20-50/month
