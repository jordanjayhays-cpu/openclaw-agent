## HOW TO READ THIS

Each prospect is scored 1-10 across **5 dimensions**. The **TOTAL** is your priority rank.

| Score | Meaning |
|-------|---------|
| 9-10  | 🔥 Top 5% — Strike immediately |
| 7-8   | ✅ High priority — Push hard |
| 5-6   | 📋 Medium — Qualify in 1 call |
| 3-4   | ❄️ Low priority — Nickels and dimes |
| 1-2   | ⛔ Skip — Wrong ICP |

---

## SCORING METHODOLOGY

Each prospect is graded on:

- **SIZE** — How many locations / members / stores does this prospect have?
- **EXPANSION** — Are they actively growing? New locations = fresh maintenance contracts (easier to close than retrofit)
- **PAIN** — Do they have visible maintenance problems? Fragmented vendors? Emergency costs?
- **DECISION SPEED** — Can one person say yes? Or is it a procurement committee?
- **MONEY** — Willingness to pay for reliability? Budget clarity?

**TOTAL = SIZE×2 + EXPANSION + PAIN + DECISION + MONEY** (max 50)
