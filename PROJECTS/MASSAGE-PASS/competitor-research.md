## Competitors Found

### Urban Company (urbancompany.com)
- **What it is:** On-demand home services marketplace (India, UAE, Aus, Europe)
- **Madrid presence:** Yes — massage services available in Madrid
- **Model:** Marketplace, per-service pricing, no subscription
- **Strength:** Established tech, large provider network
- **Weakness:** Not membership-based, no Spanish market focus, per-visit pricing

### Treatwell (treatwell.es)
- **What it is:** Beauty/wellness booking platform across Europe
- **Spain presence:** Yes, active in Spain (gift cards, per-booking)
- **Model:** Per-booking marketplace, takes commission
- **Strength:** Wide shop network, established brand
- **Weakness:** No subscription model, no membership benefits, not massage-specific

### Hammam Al Andalus Madrid
- **Type:** Traditional Moorish hammam/spa
- **Model:** Day-pass / per-visit
- **Note:** High-end experience, not subscription

### Membership-Model Startups (general research from FasterCapital)
- **Serenity Circle:** Tiered memberships (bi-weekly relaxation → intensive therapeutic)
- **Healing Hands:** Reports 40% client retention increase after switching to membership
- **Tranquil Trails:** Scheduling system syncs with members' calendars
- **Zen Zones:** Strategic pricing for diverse client base
- **Key insight:** Predictable recurring revenue + personalization = client retention
