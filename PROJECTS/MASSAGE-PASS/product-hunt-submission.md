## 📋 Extended Description

### The Problem

Paying €70+ per massage in Madrid? That's €840/month for weekly sessions. Most urban professionals either burn through their savings or skip self-care entirely.

### The Solution

**MASSAGE-PASS** is an all-you-can-massage subscription. One flat monthly fee unlocks unlimited access to 50+ partner studios across Madrid — Chueca, Chamberí, Salamanca, Malasaña, Centro, and more.

Book in seconds. No per-session pricing. No spa memberships with blackout dates. Just unlimited access to real, professional massage studios — whenever you need it.

### How It Works

1. **Subscribe** — Choose Essential (4 sessions/mo) or Premium (unlimited). Founding members get 50% off month 1.
2. **Book** — Browse partner studios, pick a time, confirm. Done in under 60 seconds.
3. **Relax** — Show up. Get massaged. Repeat.

### Who It's For

- Professionals who sit at desks 8+ hours a day
- Anyone with chronic tension, stress, or burnout
- People who want self-care without the luxury price tag
