FAQ

Which studios can I book at?

We're partnering with 50+ independent massage studios and therapists across Madrid — Chueca, Chamberí, Salamanca, Malasaña, and more. Every partner is vetted for quality. Full list visible once you're a member.

What types of massage are included?

Swedish, deep tissue, and sports massage are included at all partnered studios. Specialty treatments (hot stone, Thai, reflexology) may have a small surcharge — always shown upfront before you book.

No. Massage-Pass is month-to-month. Cancel anytime before your next billing date — no questions, no fees.

How do I book a session?

Through our app or web dashboard. Browse studios by location and availability, pick a time slot, and confirm — instant confirmation, no phone calls needed.
