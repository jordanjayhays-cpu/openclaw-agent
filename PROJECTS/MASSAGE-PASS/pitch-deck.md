## Problem
- 73% of urban professionals report chronic back/neck pain (WHO 2023)
- Booking a massage takes 15+ minutes across multiple apps and phone calls
- Therapist schedules are fragmented — no unified booking system
- Customers pay full price but get inconsistent quality and availability

---

## Solution
**MASSAGE-PASS** — A subscription-based massage booking platform that:
- Aggregates licensed massage therapists into one network
- Lets users book in under 60 seconds via web or mobile
- Offers tiered subscription plans ($49/mo basic, $89/mo premium)
- Guarantees availability with our Therapist Network model

**One subscription. Unlimited bookings. Zero friction.**
