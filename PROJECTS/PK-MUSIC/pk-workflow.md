## Overview
**Brand:** Philosophy King (PK)
**Core:** Jordan's rap music based on philosophical concepts (Nietzsche, Sapolsky, Jordan Peterson, etc.)
**Distribution:** DistroKid
**60+ tracks** in Google Drive folder

---

## Music Pipeline

### What Jordan Does (Manual)
1. Has a philosophical idea
2. Brings it to Claw
3. Goes to Suno → generates track
4. Picks best version → downloads WAV
5. Uploads to DistroKid
6. Posts to platforms

### What Claw Automates
1. **Lyrics generation** — Turn philosophical concept into rap lyrics
2. **Instrument research** — Find right instruments by philosophy origin
3. **Suno brief** — Style + mood + references for Suno
4. **Lyrics formatting** — Clean + timed for DistroKid
5. **Release strategy** — Platform timing, tags, description

## Instrument Research Database

### By Philosophy Origin

**African Philosophy:**
- Djembe, Kora, Balafon, Talking Drum
- Mbira (thumb piano)
- Genres: Afrobeat, Highlife, soukous

**Japanese Philosophy:**
- Koto, Shakuhachi, Taiko drums
- Shamisen, Biwa
- Genres: Neo-futuristic Tokyo, ambient

**Greek Philosophy:**
- Bouzouki, Saz, Oud
- Frame drum, Santouri
- Genres: Mediterranean, philosophical ambient

**Indian Philosophy:**
- Sitar, Tabla, Tanpura
- Sarod, Bansuri flute
- Genres: Sanskrit rap, spiritual hip-hop

**Chinese Philosophy:**
- Pipa, Erhu, Guzheng
- Genres: Kung fu rap, Tao flow

**Western Philosophy (Modern):**
- Piano, Synth, Trap drums
- Genres: Boom-bap, trap, lo-fi

**Latin American Philosophy:**
- Guitar, Cajas, Quena flute
- Genres: Latin rap, cumbia bass
