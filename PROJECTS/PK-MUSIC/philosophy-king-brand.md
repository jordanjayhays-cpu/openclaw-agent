# Philosophy King — Brand & Content Strategy

## The Brand

**Name:** Philosophy King (PK)
**Tagline:** "Beats that bang. Ideas that sting."
**Niche:** Philosophical hip-hop meets global wisdom
**Target:** Young creators, thinkers, entrepreneurs who want substance

## Voice

- Raw, intellectual, unapologetic
- Questions everything
- Global perspective (non-Western philosophy)
- Practical wisdom, not abstract theory
- Authenticity over performance

## Content Pillars

1. **Philosophy drops** — Song releases with intellectual context
2. **Daily wisdom** — One-liners, quotes, questions
3. **Thinking out loud** — Long-form thoughts on meaning, purpose, mortality
4. **Global thought** — African Ubuntu, Asian Tao, Latin American existentialism
5. **Anti-hustle** — Challenging productivity culture

## Platform Strategy

### Twitter/X
- **Post 3-5x daily**
- Philosophy one-liners
- Questions that make people think
- Song lyrics as hooks
- Engage with: Lex Fridman, Joe Rogan, Russ, intellectual accounts

### Instagram
- **Post daily**
- Quote cards (aesthetic)
- Behind the scenes
- Lyric snippets
- Story: thinking process

### YouTube
- **Videos 10-15 min**
- Deep dives on philosophy topics
- Song breakdowns
- Collaborations

### Discord
- Community of thinkers
- Weekly discussion threads
- Early access to music
