# PISCO Web Research — 2026-04-01

## Market Context: European Hospitality Staffing Crisis

### Key Stats (from HOTREC Jan 2026 Report)
- **~10% of hospitality roles unfilled** across EU (approx. 1 million workers short)
- **Up to 6 million worker deficit** projected by 2030 if nothing changes
- **Most affected countries**: Spain, Portugal, Greece, Italy, Romania, Czechia
- **Core pain points**: digital skills gaps, sustainability requirements, poor perception of hospitality careers, post-COVID experienced workers not returning
- **47% of European hoteliers** struggling to fill key positions (Booking.com/Statista 2025 Barometer)
- **Traditional hiring fails**: volume-based job ads = high drop-out, slow, no quality control

### Implications for PISCO
- European hotels ARE actively seeking international staffing solutions
- Filipino staff are already being recruited by competitors (Philwork → Germany, Macati, FinestAsia)
- Window is NOW — hotels are desperate and looking for new recruitment infrastructure
- Spain is one of the most affected markets — prime territory
