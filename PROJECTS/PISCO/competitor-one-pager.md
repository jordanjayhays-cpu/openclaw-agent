**"We already use a recruitment agency"**
→ Which one? (Listen) → "What happens at 90 days if the candidate leaves?" (PISCO replaces free) → "Do they offer Spanish language prep?" → Position gap.

**" Filipino staff have high turnover"**
→ That's exactly what PISCO's 92% retention rate solves. The failure other hotels see comes from poor pre-screening and no integration support. PISCO fixes both.

**"We tried international recruitment before and it was complicated"**
→ Visa, TIE, integration — PISCO handles all of it. We treat it as our problem, not yours.

**"Your fees are higher than the agency we use"**
→ Itemize: standard agency charges 20% of salary. For a €22K hire that's €4,400. PISCO flat fee: €3,000. Then add replacement costs when they leave (typically 1.5-2x placement fee in interviews, onboarding, lost revenue). PISCO's 92% retention means fewer replacements.
