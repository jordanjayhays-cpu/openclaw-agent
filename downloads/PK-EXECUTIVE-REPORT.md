## THE CONCEPT: CROSS-CULTURAL PHILOSOPHY MUSIC

### What PK Does:
1. Take a universal philosophy/theme
2. Create a song in Suno (different cultural styles)
3. Create YouTube content explaining the philosophy
4. Show how different cultures interpret the same concept

### Why This Works:
- **Suno can generate any style** — Afrobeats, Latin, Eastern, Arabic, Western
- **Philosophy is universal** — Every culture has Death, Love, Purpose, Freedom
- **Cross-cultural = differentiation** — Nobody else doing this
- **YouTube = discovery** — Philosophy videos rank in search, music keeps listeners
