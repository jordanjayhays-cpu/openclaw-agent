====================================================================

**Type:** Manila-based overseas employment agency
**Established:** 1978 (50+ years)
**Location:** Manila, Philippines

**What they do:**

- Specialize in hospitality, healthcare, and education sectors

- Documentation handling (contracts, visas, travel)
- Pre-departure orientation
- Ongoing support

**Why Filipino workers:**
- English proficiency (global business standard)
- Hospitality culture and service mindset

- NMC pathway for nurses (UK)
- International teaching credentials (schools)
