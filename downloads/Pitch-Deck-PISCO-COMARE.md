# SLIDE 6 — HOW WE DO IT NOW

## Our Approach

**Step 1: Define ICP (Ideal Customer Profile)**
- Who are the decision makers?
- What companies fit?
- What are their pain points?

**Step 2: Build Profile Partner Network**
- Recruit 5-10 real people per region
- Match their industry to our ICP
- Train on messaging guidelines

**Step 3: Research + Personalize**
- Research each target company
- Find specific decision maker
- Write personalized message per person

**Step 4: Send from Real Profiles**
- Real person sends to their network
- 1:1 messages, not spam
- Follow-up sequence built in

**Step 5: Track + Optimize**
- Reply rates
- Meeting bookings
- Client feedback loop

# SLIDE 10 — BOTTLENECKS & FIXES

## What Could Go Wrong + How We Fix It

| Bottleneck | Fix |
|------------|-----|
| **Not enough profile partners** | Offer FREE tier + network effect |
| **Low response rates** | Personalize each message, A/B test |
| **Profile partners stop responding** | Keep 2-3 backup per region |
| **LinkedIn flags accounts** | Keep volume reasonable (20 msg/week/profile) |
| **Clients expect too fast** | Set 90-day milestone expectations |
| **No meetings booked** | Follow-up sequence + phone calls |

**Our approach:**
- Start with free/super cheap partners
- Scale as we prove results
- Keep buffer partners in each region
- Monitor metrics weekly
