## WHY PHILIPPINES = NEW BALI

**The case for Philippines over Indonesia:**

| Factor | Philippines | Indonesia (Bali) |
|--------|-------------|------------------|
| **Visa** | 30 days extendable to 3 years | 30 days, harder to extend |
| **English** | Fluent everywhere | Basic English outside tourist areas |
| **Cost** | $600-1,200/month | $1,500-2,500/month (now expensive) |
| **Infrastructure** | Improving fast | Better but more expensive |
| **Safety** | Generally safe, some areas careful | Generally safe |
| **Beauty** | 7,000+ islands, incredible beaches | Incredible, but crowded |
| **Jordan's edge** | PISCO network already there | No connections |

**The timing:**
Bali just got EXPENSIVE. The same room that was $300/month in 2019 is now $800+. Nomads are looking for alternatives. Philippines is next.

- More authentic Filipino experience

**The problem Palawan solves:**

|--------|---------|

**The problem Bohol solves:**
"All the beauty of Philippines at 50% the cost. Panglao is developed enough to be easy, quiet enough to be cheap."

**Jordan angle:**
- "$500/month in the Philippines — here's how"

**The大学城 / college town nomad spot**

| Factor | Details |
|--------|---------|

**The problem Dumaguete solves:**
"I want Philippines but don't need beach clubs. Dumaguete is affordable, easy, established nomad scene."

**Jordan angle:**
- "Dumaguete — the nomad community nobody talks about"
