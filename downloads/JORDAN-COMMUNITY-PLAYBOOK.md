## WHAT communities are doing (Research Summary)

### Top Community Models 2025-2026:

**1. Subscription Communities ($5-50/month)**
- Creator posts exclusive content
- Members get: group access, tips, early content
- Examples: Tumbleweed (patreon model), Jimmy What's (subscription discords)

**2. Coaching/Accountability Communities ($100-500/month)**
- High-touch, small groups
- Weekly calls, direct access
- Examples: Every "mastermind" group ever

**3. Affiliate Communities**
- Free to join
- Creator recommends products/services
- Earns commission on everything members buy
- Examples: Most finance influencers

**4. Lead Gen Communities (B2B)**
- Free or low cost
- Members get leads/offers
- Creator monetizes via referral fees
- Examples: BD communities, real estate investor groups

**5. Content-gated Communities**
- YouTube/TikTok → community
- Exclusive Discord/WhatsApp for "real fans"
- Monetize: merchandise, courses, consulting
