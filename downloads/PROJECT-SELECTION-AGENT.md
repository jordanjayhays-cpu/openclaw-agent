# PROJECT SELECTION RESEARCH AGENT
## Scientific Evaluation Framework for Business Ideas

---

## THE PROBLEM

```
100 ideas. Which one do you actually pursue?

Most people pick based on:
- Gut feeling
- Excitement
- "It worked for someone else"

We need: Scientific selection based on real data.
```

---

## THE RESEARCH FRAMEWORK

```
EVALUATION CRITERIA (Scientific Scoring):

1. MARKET SIZE (1-10)
   → Total addressable market for this idea
   → How many people/businesses need this?

2. COMPETITIVE moat (1-10)
   → How hard is it for someone else to copy?
   → IP, relationships, expertise, location, brand

3. ENTRY BARRIER (1-10, inverse)
   → How hard to get first customer?
   → Lower = better. 1 = very easy, 10 = very hard

4. REVENUE VELOCITY (1-10)
   → How fast can you get first dollar?
   → 1 = years, 10 = days

5. MARGIN POTENTIAL (1-10)
   → What's the profit margin?
   → Services = 30-50%. SaaS = 70-90%. Product = 20-60%

6. SCALABILITY (1-10)
   → Can you grow without hitting a ceiling?
   → 1 = limited by your time. 10 = infinite

7. JORDAN FIT (1-10)
   → How well does this match Jordan's skills/network?
   → Existing relationships, language, location, industry intel

8. AI AMPLIFIABILITY (1-10)
   → How much can AI automate this?
   → Higher = more leverage for Jordan

9. RISK FACTOR (1-10, inverse)
   → What's the downside if it fails?
   → Lower = safer. 1 = no downside, 10 = life-changing loss

10. TIME TO PROFIT (1-10)
    → How quickly to $1K/month?
    → 1 = 2+ years. 10 = 30 days
```

---

## THE SCORING MATRIX

```
WEIGHTING (Jordan's priorities):
- Revenue Velocity: 25% (needs cash fast)
- Jordan Fit: 20% (existing advantage)
- Time to Profit: 20% (7 months left in MBA)
- AI Amplifiability: 15% (his unfair advantage)
- Market Size: 10% (bigger = more room)
- Margin Potential: 10% (profit matters)

TOTAL = 100%
```

---

## EVALUATION METHOD

```
For each of the 100 ideas:

STEP 1: Research
- Search: "[Business Model] market size 2026"
- Search: "[Business Model] competition landscape"
- Search: "[Business Model] success stories 2025 2026"
- Search: "[Business Model] failure rate"

STEP 2: Score each criterion (1-10 scale)
- Assign numerical scores based on research

STEP 3: Calculate weighted score
- Score × Weight = Weighted Score
- Sum all weighted scores

STEP 4: Rank
- Top 10 by weighted score
- Bottom 10 by weighted score

STEP 5: Validate
- Talk to 5 real potential customers
- Adjust scores based on real feedback

STEP 6: Recommend
- TOP 3 recommendations with full reasoning
- RISK assessment for each
- EXACT steps to validate in 1 week
```

```

Your job: Evaluate [BUSINESS IDEA] against 10 criteria using real data.

For each criterion:
1. Search for relevant data
2. Assign score 1-10
3. Cite your source

Output:
- Final score (0-100)
- Rationale for each score
- Top 3 strengths
- Top 3 weaknesses

- Risk level (LOW/MEDIUM/HIGH)
- Time to first dollar
```
