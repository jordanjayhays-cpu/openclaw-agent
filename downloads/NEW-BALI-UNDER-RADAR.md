### 🏝️ 1. LOMBOK, INDONESIA
**The "real Bali" — before it gets discovered**

| Factor | Details |
|--------|---------|
| **Monthly cost** | $600-1,000 (all-in) |
| **Internet** | 20-50 Mbps, fiber in tourist areas |
| **Nomad scene** | ~200 nomads (tiny) |
| **Visa** | Visa on arrival 30 days, extendable |
| **Safety** | Very safe, locals are incredibly friendly |
| **Weather** | Tropical, sea breezes, less humid than Bali |
| **Coworking** | 3-4 options, very basic |
| **Beaches** | Better than Bali — white sand, less crowded |
| **Rinjani volcano** | Hiking, stunning scenery |

**Why Lombok over Bali:**
- 1/3 the price
- 1/10 the nomads
- Cleaner beaches
- Just as beautiful
- Same timezone (Bali/Lombok = 0h diff)

**The problem Lombok solves:**
"Bali is too crowded and expensive. Lombok is what Bali was 15 years ago."

**Jordan angle:**
- "I found Lombok before it blew up" YouTube series
- Coliving partnership with local hotel owners (dirt cheap)
- Film: "The REAL Bali (it's called Lombok)"

**⭐ Early mover score: 9/10**
