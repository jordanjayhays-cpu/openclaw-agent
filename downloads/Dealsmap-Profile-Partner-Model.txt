DEALSMAP 2.0 — PROFILE PARTNER MODEL
=====================================
"Rent" LinkedIn profiles for B2B outreach
Realistic rates for job seekers and freelancers
Last updated: 2026-03-28

====================================================================
THE MODEL
====================================================================

You pay someone $200-500/month to use their LinkedIn profile.
They let you send messages from their profile to their connections.
You control the messaging. They get passive income.

When pitching to PISCO/COMARE:
"We have 5 people in Mexico ready to reach out from their own profiles."

Why it works:
- B2B buyers respond to in-country profiles (not cold Unknown)
- Real person, real network, real connections
- No employees = no payroll, no contracts
- Scalable — add profiles = expand regions

====================================================================
WHO TO TARGET (in order of willingness to accept lower rates)
====================================================================

1. LinkedIn "Open to Work" people
   - Most motivated, lowest rate acceptable
   - Even $200/month sounds good to someone unemployed
   - Search: "open to work" + "sales" + "[City]"

2. Recently laid off (tech, sales, marketing)
   - Mass layoffs = many qualified people desperate for income
   - Search news for "company layoffs" + target those people

3. Freelancers on Upwork/Fiverr
   - Already used to variable income
   - Willing to accept less for steady work

4. Job board applicants
   - People who applied to jobs = actively searching
   - Cold email them with offer

5. University students/graduates
   - Need income, flexible hours
   - Good for entry-level ICP targets

====================================================================
COMPENSATION — REALISTIC RATES
====================================================================

Target: People looking for work = they accept lower rates

Option A: Flat Monthly (simplest)
- Job seeker / unemployed: $200-400/month
- Freelancer looking for work: $300-500/month
- Active freelancer: $400-700/month
- Covers: 20-30 messages per week

Option B: Per Message (lower risk)
- $1-3 per message sent
- $5 per reply received
- $25 per meeting booked

Option C: Hybrid (recommended)
- $150-250/month base
- $2 per message sent
- $10 per reply received
- $50 per meeting booked

Realistic pitch to them:
"You let me use your profile for 20 messages/week = $400/month
 You don't do anything except approve what I send."

====================================================================
LEGAL/ETHICAL NOTES
====================================================================

LinkedIn ToS: Gray area — real human sending messages is allowed
- Don't mass message their entire network
- Keep volume reasonable (20-30 per week)
- Don't post publicly as them
- Profile owner consents = you have permission

What IS allowed:
- You write, they review and send
- They're a contractor, not employee
- 1:1 messages only

What NOT allowed:
- Automated sending (against ToS)
- Public posts as them
- Spam to their entire network
