DEALSMAP 2.0 — PROFILE PARTNER MODEL
=====================================
"Rent" LinkedIn profiles for B2B outreach
Last updated: 2026-03-28

====================================================================
THE MODEL
====================================================================

You pay someone $500-1,500/month to "join" your company.
They let you use their LinkedIn profile for outreach.
You control the messaging. They get passive income.

When pitching to PISCO/COMARE:
"We have 5 people in Mexico ready to reach out from their own profiles."

Why it works:
- B2B buyers respond to in-country profiles (not cold Unknown)
- LinkedIn accepts it (real person, real network)
- Scalable — add profiles = expand regions
- No employees = no payroll, no contracts

====================================================================
WHAT TO TELL THEM
====================================================================

"It's simple:
- You stay at your current job
- You let us use your LinkedIn profile to send messages on our behalf
- We send messages WE write, from YOUR profile, to YOUR connections
- You get paid $X/month for 10-20 hours/month of 'passive' work
- We never post as you — only send messages
- You can stop anytime"

Compensation ideas:
- Base: $500-1,000/month per profile
- Per message: $5-10 per reply received
- Per meeting: $50-100 per meeting booked

====================================================================
IDEAL PROFILE CRITERIA
====================================================================

Good profile partner:
- 500+ LinkedIn connections
- Works in: sales, business development, recruitment, HR, management
- Has decision-maker connections in their region
- NOT: current recruitment agency owner (conflict)
- English or bilingual (for US/LATAM)
- Spanish + English (for Spain/LATAM)

Bad profile partner:
- <100 connections (not enough reach)
- Unemployed (will post as you, create noise)
- Already a recruiter/sales rep (conflicts)
- Aggressive networker (will notice if you spam)

Target demographics:
- Mid-level professionals (5-15 years experience)
- Age 28-45
- Job title: Sales Manager, BD Director, HR Manager, Recruiter, Account Manager
- Industry: Tech, Finance, Healthcare, Hospitality (matches ICPs)

- Profile owner does nothing except approve messages

Option B: Usage-Based
- $300/month base
- $5 per message sent

- $10 per reply

====================================================================
LEGAL/ETHICAL CONSIDERATIONS
====================================================================

ToS Risk:
- LinkedIn ToS prohibits scraping and automated messaging
- Using real profiles is a gray area — real humans sending messages
- Profile owner consents = you have permission
- Don't use to spam — LinkedIn will flag if too many reports

What to avoid:
- Don't post as them (only send 1:1 messages)
- Don't mass-message their entire network
- Keep volume reasonable (20-30 messages/week/profile)
- Don't message their direct connections about competing services

What IS allowed:
- You write the message, they review and send
- Or you have them forward messages to their network
- They're a "contractor" with a service agreement

Service Agreement (simple):
"Profile Partner Agreement
- [Name] grants [Company] permission to send LinkedIn messages
  from their personal profile on their behalf
- [Company] will not post publicly or send spam
- [Company] pays $[Amount] monthly
- Either party can terminate with 30 days notice"
