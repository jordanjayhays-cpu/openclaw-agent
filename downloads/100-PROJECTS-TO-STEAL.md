# 100 PROJECTS TO STEAL
## Competitive Research: What's Working → What's Next

---

## THE METHOD

```
Research: What are other people building successfully?
Filter: Is this replicable in another niche or country?
Opportunity: Where does Jordan's skills + AI fit?

100 projects organized by:
A) SERVICE businesses (labor arbitrage)
B) SAAS businesses (software eats the world)
C) CONTENT businesses (attention = money)
D) AGENCY businesses (expertise + execution)
E) PRODUCT businesses (info, templates, tools)
F) MARKETPLACE businesses (matching buyers + sellers)
```

---

## SECTION A: SERVICE BUSINESSES

---
