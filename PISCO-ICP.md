## PISCO — ICP & Campaign Plan

### Who PISCO Serves (from company profile)
Filipino overseas employment agency. Deploys Filipino workers globally. 50 years in business.

### PISCO's Ideal Customer Profile

**Geography targets:**
- 🇩🇪 **Germany** — hospitality, healthcare, manufacturing labor shortages
- 🇪🇸 **Spain** — tourism/hospitality industry
- 🇵🇹 **Portugal** — construction, hospitality
- 🇬🇧 **UK** — NHS healthcare workers, care homes
- 🇦🇺 **Australia** — hospitality, agriculture

**Industry targets:**

| Industry | Why they need PISCO |
|----------|---------------------|
| **Hotels & Hospitality** | High turnover, hard to hire locally, Filipino workers have hospitality culture fit |
| **Private Healthcare** | Nurses, care workers for aging population |
| **Construction** | Labor shortages across Europe |
| **Manufacturing** | Factory workers, technicians |
| **Facilities Management** | Commercial cleaning, maintenance |

**Who to contact:**
- HR Directors
- Operations Directors  
- Recruitment Managers
- CEOs (small-mid companies)

### PISCO's Pitch
> "We handle end-to-end recruitment of Filipino workers — screening, visas, deployment, compliance. You get pre-qualified, English-speaking workers at competitive rates."

### MVP Campaign Steps

**Week 1 — Germany Manufacturing**
1. I research 20 German manufacturing companies (Medium-large, have overseas hiring history)
2. I find contacts via LinkedIn
3. You send connection requests + initial InMail
4. I draft personalized emails
5. You send via your email

**Sequence:**
```
Day 1: Connection request (LinkedIn)
Day 3: Follow-up (if no response)
Day 7: Email #1 — personalized intro
Day 10: Follow-up email
Day 14: LinkedIn message
Day 21: Break-up email
```
