# Booking Platform Research — MassagePass Feature Gaps

**Researched:** Mindbody, Booksy, Fresha, Square Appointments, Jane App
**Date:** 2026-04-26

---

## What top booking platforms offer

### Fresha (strongest competitor for massage/spa)
**Pricing:** Free for businesses, takes per-booking fee only from marketplace discovery. No monthly fee.
**Key features:**
- Online booking + real-time calendar
- SMS + email automated reminders
- Payment processing (upfront deposits, no-show protection)
- Global marketplace (120,000+ businesses)
- Client referrals + promotional discounts
- Marketing campaigns (automated)
- Inventory management
- Team scheduling
- Real-time analytics
- No long-term contract

### Jane App (best UX for wellness)
**Pricing:** Per-practitioner, affordable
**Key features:**
- Online booking
- Telehealth
- AI Scribe (auto-document sessions)
- Intake forms
- Email + SMS reminders
- Insurance billing (direct bill to insurers)
- Simple check-in
- Reporting & analytics
- Credit card billing + receipts

### Mindbody (enterprise, expensive)
**Key features:**
- All-in-one (staff, clients, POS, marketing)
- **Commissions 15-20%** on bookings
- High monthly cost
- Consumer app + marketplace
- Rewards/loyalty program

### Booksy (beauty-focused, expand to massage)
**Key features:**
- Online booking
- Client management
- Marketing tools
- Multi-location support
- Reviews management
