## 🎯 RECOMMENDED PITCH OPENING FOR IBERDROLA DATATHON

### Option A: The Problem Opener
```
"Iberdrola has [X,XXX] charging points across Spain. 
But here's the problem: [percentage] of them are underutilized 
because they're in the wrong locations.

We used your smart mobility data to answer one question: 
'Where should the next 100 chargers go?'"

(Transition to methodology + results)
```

### Option B: The "What If" Opener
```
"What if we could predict where every EV charging bottleneck 
will occur in Spain — before it happens?

Iberdrola collects [X]TB of charging data daily. 
You use [Y]% of it. 

The other [100-Y]% contains the answer. Here's what we found."
```

### Option C: The Story Opener
```
"Three months ago, an EV driver in Bilbao spent 47 minutes 
looking for a charging station. The station was full.

The data was there. We just weren't looking.

Today, I'll show you how to never miss a data point like that again."
```

### Option D: The Statistic Opener (Recommended)
```
"Spain needs [X] more public EV charging points to meet 
2025 EU targets. But building them costs [€Y].

Our analysis shows Iberdrola could optimize existing infrastructure 
to serve [Z]% more drivers WITHOUT building a single new charger.

Here's how."
```
