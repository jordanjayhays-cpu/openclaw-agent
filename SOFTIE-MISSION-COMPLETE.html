📋 Your Mission

In this simulation game, you are part of the Leveraged Finance (LevFin) department within your Investment Bank. LevFin specializes in providing debt financing solutions to corporates.

Your primary aim is to become the lender for a company currently refinancing its capital structure by successfully closing the debt funding deal, while your secondary objective is to secure favorable funding terms.

You will compete with other lenders, all of whom have been mandated to secure a debt funding deal with favorable terms. At the end of the simulation, your performance will be evaluated and compared to that of other lender teams who have been assigned the same tasks.

Your ability to negotiate effectively and secure advantageous funding terms will determine your success.

📋 Task Checklist

  • Watch the overview video
  • Read mission, case study, and term guidance (~15 min)
  • Team discussion — consolidate understanding
  • Divide coverage of terms and input desired outcomes
  • Team discussion — focus on: issuer entity, loan amount, financial covenant
  • Read negotiation guidance
  • Prepare arguments and define negotiation strategy

⚠️ Key Risk Flags to Remember

  • EBITDA "adjustments" — €10m above reported (question this)
  • Goodwill declined €280m → €200m in one year
  • "Advanced discussions" for €20m acquisition — unsigned
  • PIK Note is unsecured — expensive existing debt
  • SaaS contracts = churn risk if customers leave

🏆 Winning Strategy

  • Close the deal (primary objective)
  • Price risk appropriately — don't undercut just to win
  • Use covenants as leverage, not just price
  • Reference comparable deals (CNNX 5.6x, SaaSy 6.0x)
  • Get security over IP and shares — your protection
  • Define clear walk-away points before negotiating

💡 Pro Tips for the Sim

  • You go first: Submit your best terms first — anchor the negotiation
  • Other lenders respond: Watch what they do, then adjust
  • Don't give everything: Every concession should get something in return
  • Financial covenant is KEY: Spend most time here — it's your early warning system
