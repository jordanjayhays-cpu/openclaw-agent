## Why Regional Mid-Market?

- **Decision makers are accessible** — Owner/GM makes the call same day
- **No procurement process** — No 6-month vendor onboarding
- **Relationship matters** — Jordan can actually sell this
- **Right-sized contracts** — $2K-10K/month per client, not enterprise deals
- **Reference clients build case studies** — Use one to get the next

**COMARE's sweet spot:** 20-200 locations in Mexico City metro. Companies tired of managing 10+ vendors.
