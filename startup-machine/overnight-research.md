## 2. Pricing Intelligence — Gym Maintenance in Mexico City

### HVAC
- **Monthly range:** $500–$2,000 MXN/month per unit (~$25–$100 USD)
- **Full HVAC contract (multiple units):** $2,000–$8,000 MXN/month (~$100–$400 USD)
- **Note:** Gyms typically have 4–10 AC units per location. Large Smart Fit/Bodytech locations can have 15–20 units.
- **Typical contract type:** Fixed monthly retainer or per-visit fee

### Plumbing
- **Per visit (emergency):** $500–$2,000 MXN ($25–$100 USD)
- **Monthly retainer (small gym):** $1,500–$3,000 MXN ($75–$150 USD)
- **Common issues:** Shower stalls (high usage = leaks), locker room plumbing, toilet facilities, pool pumps
- **Gym-specific pain point:** Members complain immediately about wet floors/broken showers = churn risk

### Pool Maintenance (Bodytech/gyms with aquatic facilities)
- **Monthly pool service contract:** $3,000–$10,000 MXN/month (~$150–$500 USD)
- **Includes:** Chemical balance, pump maintenance, vacuuming, filter cleaning
- **Additional:** Pump replacement = $5,000–$20,000 MXN one-time
- **Frequency:** 2–4 visits/week for high-use gym pools

### Combined / All-in Maintenance Contract Estimates
Based on gym size:

| Gym Size | Locations | Est. Monthly Contract |
|----------|-----------|----------------------|
| Small (1 location, < 500m²) | 1 | $3,000–$6,000 MXN (~$150–$300 USD) |
| Medium (chain, 5–20 locations) | 5–20 | $1,500–$3,000 MXN/location ($75–$150 USD) |
| Large (Smart Fit/Bodytech tier) | 50–200+ | $800–$2,500 MXN/location ($40–$125 USD) — but volume discount |

**Key insight for COMARE pitch:**
- COMARE's sweet spot listed in pitch: $500–$1,500/location for small, $1,500–$3,000 for medium, $3,000–$10,000 for large
- Gym chains with 20+ locations in CDMX → could represent $30,000–$200,000 MXN/month in contract value
- Price objection expected: gyms are margin-conscious. Frame as "one invoice, one number, zero vendor chaos" not just maintenance.
