🧠 Core Methodologies

🔄 Three-Circle Model

The foundational family business framework

  • Family: Relationships, values, ownership expectations
  • Business: Strategy, structure, competition
  • Ownership: Governance, shareholder rights, exits

📋 Family Constitution

The core artifact — written rules for the family

  • Family's shared values and vision
  • Who can work in the business (and who can't)
  • How decisions are made
  • How ownership transfers
  • How family members are compensated
  • Conflict resolution processes

🎯 Five Dimensions of Continuity (FFI)

  • Governance: Boards, councils, decision-making structures
  • Strategy: Long-term vision and competitive positioning
  • Entrepreneurship: Innovation and new venture creation
  • Leadership: Succession and capability development
  • Ownership: Shareholder engagement and stewardship

📊 Next-Gen Readiness Assessment

  • Capability (do they have the skills?)
  • Commitment (do they want this?)
  • Character (can they be trusted?)
  • Culture fit (will they preserve the legacy?)
  • Capital (are they financially independent?)

⚖️ Conflict Management Framework

  • Identify: Map the conflict lines
  • Separate: Family issues vs. business issues
  • Structure: Governance and processes
  • Mediate: Facilitate productive conversations
  • Document: Capture agreements in writing

📈 Growth Levers for Family Businesses

  • Professionalize: Systems, processes, outside talent
  • Diversify: New markets, products, revenue streams
  • Digitize: Modernize operations and experience
  • Restructure: Optimize organizational design
  • Brand: Build equity beyond founder reputation

🤝 Trust-Building with Family Businesses

  • Long-term relationships over quick wins
  • Emotional ownership — it's personal
  • Absolute discretion — confidentiality is sacred
  • Respect the founder's legacy
  • Be patient — consensus takes time
  • Never position yourself as "fixing" their family
