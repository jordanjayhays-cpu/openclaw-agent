Family Business Growth Frameworks

📊 Business Development Approach

  • Diagnose: Understand the family business lifecycle stage
  • Map: Identify key decision-makers and governance structure
  • Position: Frame your value proposition for family-owned context
  • Grow: Implement systems for sustainable scaling

⚖️ Conflict Management Framework

  • Identify: Map the conflict lines (family vs business? generation vs generation?)
  • Separate: Distinguish family issues from business issues
  • Structure: Establish clear governance and decision-making processes
  • Mediate: Facilitate productive conversations with defined outcomes
  • Document: Capture agreements and follow-up mechanisms

👨‍👩‍👧‍👦 Family Governance Models

  • Family Council: Formal family decision-making body
  • Board of Directors: External perspectives + family representation
  • Family Constitution: Written values, vision, and rules
  • Succession Protocol: Clear process for leadership transition

📈 Growth Levers for Family Businesses

  • Professionalize: Systems, processes, outside talent
  • Diversify: New markets, products, or revenue streams
  • Digitize: Modernize operations and customer experience
  • Restructure: Optimize organizational design
  • Brand: Build brand equity beyond founder reputation

🤝 Trust-Building with Family Businesses

  • They value long-term relationships over quick wins
  • Show understanding of emotional ownership (it's personal)
  • Demonstrate discretion — confidentiality is critical
  • Respect the founder's legacy while pushing for growth
  • Be patient — consensus-building takes time

💼 BD Playbook for Family Biz

  • Entry Point: Succession planning is the #1 hook — every family biz faces it
  • Pain Points: Sibling conflict, professionalizing without losing culture, exit planning
  • Starter Offer: "Family Business Health Check" — 2-hour assessment, €500-1000
  • Upsell: Implementation work, ongoing advisory retainer
  • Referrals: Accountants and lawyers are gold — they already work with family businesses
