📋 Term-by-Term Strategy & Targets

🏢 Issuer Entity Max Score

Who is the actual borrower? SPV or OpCo?
🎯 Target: Softie NV (HoldCo level = riskier = higher yield needed)
Argument: If below OpCo, demand 50-100bps premium for structural subordination

💰 Loan Amount Your Call

What's the refinance quantum?
🎯 Target: Smaller = less risk. Ask: "Is this refinancing or additional raise?"
Argument: If PE is putting in equity, you have more leverage on terms

📈 Interest Rate MAXIMIZE

Your primary return — price the risk
🎯 Target: EURIBOR + 450-550bps (given leverage + sector)
Argument: Comps trade at 5-6x, Softie at 10x implies growth premium. Argue: "Your valuation is stretched — we need compensating yield"
💡: Reference CNNX (5.6x) and SaaSy (6.0x) — you're taking more risk

📅 Maturity SHORTER = BETTER

tenor = 4.5yr PIK Note already exists
🎯 Target: 3-4 years max
Argument: "4.5yr PIK Note is already out the door. Your new debt should mature before or at same time. We won't extend beyond [date]."
💡: If you must go longer, demand 25bps per year step-up

🔄 Amortization MAXIMIZE CASH PAY

Cash paydown schedule — prefer faster repayment
🎯 Target: 1-2% p.a. amortization + 50% excess cash flow sweep
Argument: "SaaS companies burn cash. We want amortization to ensure you're not dependent on refinancing to repay us"
Concede on: If borrower argues cash constraints, reduce to 0.5% with step-up provisions

⏰ Prepayment Option BALANCED

Make-whole vs call protection
🎯 Target: Soft call for 12 months at 102, then 101, then par
Argument: "We need time to reinvest proceeds. 12-month soft call protects our refinancing risk"
Concede on: Step-down schedule if borrower pushes back on lockout period

🛡️ Guarantees DEMAND FULL

Who guarantees the loan?
🎯 Target: Full guarantee from OpCo (Softie NV) + all material subsidiaries
Argument: "We're lending to HoldCo structure. We need downstream guarantees to have real recourse"
💡: If they refuse, reduce loan size or increase pricing by 50bps

📊 Seniority SENIOR TO PIK

Where in capital structure?
🎯 Target: Senior secured — ranking ahead of €200m PIK Note
Argument: "PIK Note is unsecured. New money = senior secured treatment required"
Counter: If PIK Note holders object, offer "last-out" senior secured at 25bps discount

🔒 Security MAXIMIZE COLLATERAL

What assets secure the loan?
🎯 Target: First ranking security over all assets (shares of OpCo + all material IP)
Argument: "Goodwill is €200m today, €280m last year. IP is declining — we need security to protect against impairment"
Critical: Get share pledges on Softie Midco NV + Softie Holdco NV

✅ Borrower Reps & Warranties STANDARD + ADDITIONS

Bring-down of representations
🎯 Target: Market standard + specific "goodwill not impaired" + "no material contracts terminated"
Argument: "Given the IP valuation decline, we need explicit warranty that no impairment is pending"
💡: Add "no undisclosed liabilities" from SaaP acquisition

📋 Conditions Precedent DEMAND MORE

Conditions before funding — use as leverage
🎯 Target: CP list should include:
• Audited financials (not management accounts)
• Solvency opinion
• IP valuation report (not declining)
• Evidence of SaaP synergies realized
Argument: "Given €10m adjustments, we'll only fund on audited numbers"

👍 Affirmative Covenants MARKET STANDARD

Ongoing obligations — don't over-negotiate here
🎯 Target: Standard reporting (monthly/quarterly), but add:
• SaaS ARR disclosure
• Churn rate reporting
• IP valuation update annually
Argument: "SaaS metrics are key to your story. We need visibility on recurring revenue quality"

🚫 Negative Covenants TIGHTEN MAX

Restricted activities — protect your position
🎯 Target: Include:
• No additional debt > €5m without consent
• No acquisitions without consent (esp. the €20m rumored deal)
• No asset sales > €2m without consent
• No dividend payments while leverage > 3x
Argument: "We know about the €20m acquisition opportunity. Any M&A needs lender consent"

📐 Financial Covenant (Leverage) TIGHTEST YOU CAN GET

THIS IS THE KEY TERM — spend most time here
🎯 Target: Leverage ≤ 3.5x Total Net Debt / EBITDA
Definition: Use Reported EBITDA (€70m), not Adjusted (€80m)
Equity Cure: Allow max 2x per year, 3% max each, 5% lifetime cap
Testing: Quarterly, with 20% cure right cushion
Argument: "Adjusted EBITDA is €10m above reported. We're pricing on real numbers"
⚠️ FIGHT HARD: If they want 4x+, demand 25-50bps additional margin

🔄 Change of Control PROTECT YOUR VOTE

What happens if PE sponsor exits?
🎯 Target: Mandatory prepayment at par + 101 call premium upon CoC
Argument: "PE sponsor exit = likely recap. We need to be able to demand repayment or re-price at new leverage"
Alternative: Lender consent required for CoC (not just notification)

❌ Events of Default STANDARD + ADDITIONS

Cross-default, payment default, financial covenant breach
🎯 Target: Market standard + add:
• Goodwill impairment > €20m = EoD
• IP valuation < €150m = EoD
• Material SaaS contract loss > 20% ARR
Argument: "Given IP volatility, we need early warning triggers"

💸 Default Rate MAXIMIZE PENALTY

Interest rate during default period
🎯 Target: P+300bps (prevailing rate + 3%) with 5% floor
Argument: "We need meaningful penalty to discourage strategic default"
💡: This is standard — don't waste negotiating capital here

💳 Fees & Expenses MAXIMIZE UPFRONT

Arrangement fee, underwriting fee, legal costs
🎯 Target:
• Arrangement fee: 2.0-2.5% upfront
• Underwriting fee: 1.0%
• Legal expenses: borrower pays regardless
Argument: "We're taking leverage risk. Upfront fees compensate for structuring cost"

🎯 Quick Reference — Your Walk-Away Points

✅ Walk-Away IF:

• Interest rate < EURIBOR + 400bps
• Financial covenant > 4.0x leverage
• No security over IP/shares
• Maturity > 5 years without premium
• Arrangement fee < 1.5%

⚠️ Negotiate IF:

• Interest rate 400-500bps (split the diff)
• Covenant 3.5-4.0x (add equity cure)
• Partial security on IP
• 4-5 year maturity with step-up
• Fee 1.5-2.0%

🏆 Acceptable Range:

• IR: EURIBOR + 450-500bps
• Covenant: 3.5-4.0x
• Security: IP + shares
• Maturity: 3.5-4.5 years
• Fee: 2.0-2.5%

🚨 Red Lines — Don't Cross:

• Covenant using Adjusted EBITDA
• No equity cure right
• Goodwill excluded from security
• PIK Note outranks your debt
• No CoC protection

📊 Comparable-Backed Arguments

Use These in Negotiation:

"Look at CNNX (5.6x) and SaaSy (6.0x):" Comps with similar SaaS models trade at 5-6x leverage. If Softie wants financing at comparable terms, it needs to justify pricing above comps given EBITDA quality concerns.

"Your EV/EBITDA is 10x vs 5.8x comps:" Either your growth story is exceptional (prove it) or your EBITDA is inflated (we'll use reported numbers).

"WeCloud trades at 6.1x with €133m cash:" You have €52m cash against €225m existing debt. Your net debt position is worse than comparables.

"Al-Mind has -3 EBITDA but 5.5x:" Even loss-making comps price better. Your €10m "adjustments" don't inspire confidence.
