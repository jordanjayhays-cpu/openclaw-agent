I have a confession: I've never really "used" my LinkedIn network.

I look at it. I scroll it. I maybe post something once a quarter.

Meanwhile, businesses are desperately trying to reach people exactly like me and you — professionals with relevant experience, in relevant industries, connected to relevant people.

And they can't get through. Cold outreach fails. InMail is ignored. Ads don't work.

So I asked myself: what if your LinkedIn profile was an asset someone could rent?

That's ProfileStack.

Here's how it works:
→ A business needs to reach VPs of Engineering at SaaS companies
→ They rent MY LinkedIn profile for outreach
→ I review and approve every message they want to send
→ They handle the outreach through the platform
→ I get paid €75–150/month — for essentially doing nothing

I keep full control. No one logs into my account. No one impersonates me. I just... let them use my professional reputation.

Right now I'm building the first wave of ProfileStack and looking for 10 profile owners to launch with.

Ideal if you:
✓ Have 3+ years of experience in your industry
✓ Have 500+ connections
✓ Don't post/sell on LinkedIn much (your network is "quiet")
✓ Want a genuinely passive €75–150/month

Not ideal if you:
✗ Are actively selling or recruiting on LinkedIn
✗ Only have 50 connections

No cost to join. No commitment. I just need people who want to test this with me.

Drop a comment "interested" or DM me — let's talk.

And if you know someone who'd be perfect for this, tag them. 🙏
