## Character Count Guide

| Type | Target |
|------|--------|
| Cold outreach | 200-280 chars |
| Warm intro | 180-260 chars |
| Follow-up 1 | 150-220 chars |
| Follow-up 2-3 | 120-180 chars |

**LinkedIn mobile preview shows ~140 chars.** Put the hook and CTA in the first two lines.
