# Content Brainstorm Skill

## Description
Generate video/podcast/content ideas for Jordan's projects and interests.

## Triggers
- "brainstorm content for [project]"
- "give me video ideas"
- "content topic about [topic]"
- "what should I make content about"
- "generate content for [platform]"

## Jordan's Content Pillars
1. **Neurotech** — Neuralink, longevity, psychedelics, consciousness, AI+brain
2. **Business/BD** — PISCO, COMARE, Dealsmap 2.0, outbound sales
3. **Entrepreneurship** — Building businesses, IMBA experience
4. **Productivity** — Life optimization, science-backed improvements
5. **Digital Nomad** — New Bali, location independence

## For Neurotech Content
### Video Formats
- Deep dive (20-30 min): Technical explainers
- Reaction (15-20 min): News, studies, announcements
- Interview style (30-60 min): Scientists, founders
- Personal experiment (15-25 min): Testing biohacks, protocols

### Hook Formulas
- "I watched [X] and here's what happened"
- "The truth about [X] that nobody talks about"
- "How [X] will change [industry]"
- "I spent $X on [X] — was it worth it?"
- "Why [X] is the next [Y]"

### Trending Angles
- Neuralink first human results
- Bryan Johnson Blueprint protocol updates
- New longevity studies
- Psychedelic therapy breakthroughs
- AI + consciousness research

## For Business/BD Content
### Video Formats
- Case study: "How I got [result] for [client]"
- Tutorial: "How to do [X] outreach"
- Strategy: "The [Y] method for [Z]"
- Behind the scenes: "Building [project] in real time"

### Hook Formulas
- "I got [X] replies using this method"
- "The outreach strategy that [worked/failed]"
- "How to [X] without [common mistake]"

## For Productivity Content
### Video Formats
- Science breakdown (15-20 min)
- Personal experiment (10-15 min)
- Routine tour (5-10 min)
- Tool review (10-15 min)

## Output Format
```
# Content Ideas: [Topic]

## Video 1: [Title]
- Format: [Deep dive/Reaction/Tutorial/etc]
- Length: [X] minutes
- Hook: "[Hook formula]"
- Key points: [3-5 bullets]
- Thumbnail: [Visual idea]

## Video 2: [Title]
...

## Hook Variations
- "[Hook 1]"
- "[Hook 2]"
- "[Hook 3]"
```
