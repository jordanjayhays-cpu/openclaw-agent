# Ego Death Speech Research

**Date started:** 2026-04-19
**Status:** Ongoing

## Key Sources Identified

### Wikipedia - Ego Death (good overview)
- William James coined "self-surrender"
- Jungian psychology = "psychic death"
- Joseph Campbell's Hero's Journey = ego death as phase 2 (self-surrender + transition)
- Leary/Metzner/Alpert 1964: ego death = "complete transcendence — beyond words, beyond spacetime, beyond self. No visions, no sense of self, no thoughts. Only pure awareness and ecstatic freedom."
- Stanislav Grof: "instant merciless destruction of all previous reference points"
- Eckhart Tolle: ego as accumulation of thoughts/identifications

### Terence McKenna
- "Timothy Leary of the '90s"
- Known for ego death in DMT/psychedelic context
- Countercultural icon, lecturer
- Key concept: "stoned ape theory" + novelty theory
- Famous for high-energy, visionary, provocative delivery
- Speeches often available on YouTube: "Terence McKenna DMT experience"

### Alan Watts
- "Why You Are Not The Doer" - most cited short piece on ego death
- Playful, paradox-heavy style
- "skin-encapsulated ego" - Grof cites this exact phrase

### Ram Dass
- "Be Here Now" era
- More devotional, compassionate tone
- Key talks: "ego, love, and awareness"

### Jiddu Krishnamurti
- Raw, uncompromising
- No comfort - "see the structure of the self"
- Direct, non-dual

### Nisargadatta Maharaj
- "I Am That" - blunt, non-dual
- "You are not the doer"

### Ram Dass
- Born Richard Alpert, Harvard psychologist
- Collaborated with Timothy Leary on psychedelic research
- 1967: went to India, became disciple of Neem Karoli Baba, renamed Ram Dass
- "Be Here Now" (1971) - seminal book on Eastern spirituality for West
- Spiritual teacher in Hindu lineage
- Key message: "Be Here Now" +compassionate awareness
- Stroke in 1997 left him with expressive aphasia - saw it as grace
- Died 2019 in Maui
