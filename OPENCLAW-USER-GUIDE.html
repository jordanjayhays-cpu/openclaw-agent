💬 How to Talk to Axton

The 3 Rules:

1. Be Direct

❌ "Can you maybe possibly..."
✅ "Do this"

2. Give Context Once

Tell me about your projects, goals, preferences — I'll remember.

3. Give Me Tasks, Not Questions

❌ "What's my schedule?"
✅ "Send me my schedule for today"
