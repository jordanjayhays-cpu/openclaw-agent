# SUCCESS_LOG.md — What Worked

## Outreach
- **LinkedIn outreach with personalization** — Higher response rate than generic messages
- **Direct LinkedIn messages** — Better than email for initial contact
- **Case studies in outreach** — "We placed X at Y in Z days" works as proof
- **Subagent parallel research** — Faster than manual research

## System
- **GitHub as backup** — Files survive resets when memory fails
- **Subagents for research tasks** — Parallelize, don't sequentialize
- **Auto-save after significant changes** — Prevents lost work
- **Draft → Review → Execute workflow** — Jordan approves before external actions

## Communication
- **Copy-paste ready messages** — Jordan wants plain text, not file links
- **Short responses** — Twitter-length when possible
- **One action per message** — Don't overwhelm

## Contacts/CRM
- **CONTEXT.md tracking** — All contacts in one place with warmth rating
- **LinkedIn profiles saved** — Direct URLs, not just names
