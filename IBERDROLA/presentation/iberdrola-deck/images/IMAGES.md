## Notes for Presentation Designer

1. **Match Netflix style:** Dark backgrounds, white text, large stats
2. **Iberdrola green (#00974C)** as accent color
3. **Blur images** slightly for text overlay readability
4. **Use gradients** over photos for title slides
5. **Keep charts simple** — one main insight per slide
