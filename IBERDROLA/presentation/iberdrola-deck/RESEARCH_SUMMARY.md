# Iberdrola Presentation Research Summary
**Compiled:** April 21, 2026

## Iberdrola Company Info
- Spain's largest electricity utility
- Smart Mobility Lab in Bilbao
- Partnership with bp pulse for ultrafast charging
- Largest ultra-fast charging hub in Southern Europe (Elche, 4MW, 400kW chargers)
- Charging powers: 50kW (fast), 150kW (super-fast), 350kW (ultra-fast)

## EU Requirements for Spain
- 420,000 public charging points by 2030
- 36% of new cars must be EV by 2030
- Spain currently has ~50,000-54,000 chargers
- Gap: ~370,000 chargers needed
- Rate required: 92,500/year (8x current rate)

## Our Data (from Iberdrola dataset)
- 627 stations analyzed
- 91.5% congested
- 568 new stations proposed
- Payback: 1.9 years avg (with 30% EU grants)
- 1.54M projected EVs by 2027

## JTBD Customer Insights
- 8,350 data points analyzed
- Top pain points: App/Digital, Pricing, Route Planning, Charging Speed
- Need: Reliability + Smart Placement

## Story Structure (50%)
1. The Problem (EU targets vs reality)
2. The Data (our analysis)
3. The Solution (proposed stations)
4. The Impact (payback, customer trust)
5. The Ask (support for implementation)
